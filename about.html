Olenka Yuen


I’m Olenka Yuen, a 3D creator from Montreal, Quebec.
In a few months, I will receive a bachelor’s of Computation Arts from Concordia University.


I have a fondness for cute, chunky, and hand-painted worlds. Specializing in 3D modeling, I use tools like Blender 3D, Figma, Paint Tool SAI, Ibis Paint, and Adobe After Effects to conceptualize and bring my art to life.


I also draw illustrations of my favorite media and explore new creative expressions during my free time.
