Olenka Yuen


I’m Olenka Yuen, a 3D creator from Montreal, Quebec with a bachelor’s in Computation Arts from Concordia University.


I have a passion for crafting cute, chunky, and hand-painted worlds. Specializing in 3D modeling, I bring my art to life using tools like Blender 3D, Figma, Paint Tool SAI, Ibis Paint, and Adobe After Effects.


Beyond 3D, I also enjoy illustrating my favorite media and exploring new creative expressions in my free time. I’m always looking for opportunities to contribute to projects that embrace imaginative and stylized aesthetics.
